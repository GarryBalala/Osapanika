Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Payday Loan

What is a payday loan?

A payday loan is a short-term loan designed to provide quick cash for immediate expenses, typically repaid on your next payday. It helps bridge temporary cash flow gaps between paychecks.

Who is eligible for a payday loan?

Individuals with a stable income who can provide proof of employment (letter from employer and recent payslip) and a valid national ID are eligible. You must be at least 18 years old.

What documents are required?

You'll need three documents: (1) A letter from your employer confirming your employment status, (2) A recent payslip showing your income, and (3) A valid national ID.

How long does it take to get the money?

Once approved, you can receive your funds in just 24 hours. The entire process from application to disbursement is designed to be as quick as possible.

When do I need to repay the loan?

The loan is typically repaid in full on your next payday, which is usually within one month. The exact repayment date will be agreed upon when you take out the loan.
